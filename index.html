About Us

Our Story

Cause we make the best delicacies in town, that's why!

Welcome to AsoRock Kitchen , where culinary excellence meets warm hospitality in the heart of Lagos Nigeria. Nestled Chevron Hotel, our restaurant is a beloved destination for those who appreciate Nigerian dishes and foreign dishes. At AsoRock Kitchen we pride ourselves on crafting memorable dining experiences. Our talented team of chefs, led by Chef Dammy of Ekiti, meticulously selects the finest ingredients to create dishes that blend traditional recipes with a modern twist. Whether you're joining us for a casual lunch, an intimate dinner, or a celebratory event, every plate is prepared with passion and creativity.

Working Hours

As a chef, I believe that food is more than mere sustenance. It's a canvas for creativity, a journey of flavors, and a testament to the joy of sharing. Every dish tells a story, weaving together ingredients with technique to evoke emotions and memories. Cooking is my way of expressing love and passion, inviting others to experience the artistry and soul that goes into each plate. It's a privilege to nourish and delight, transforming simple ingredients into moments of culinary delight that linger on the palate and in the heart.

Chef Dammy - Restaurant Chef
